Notes

  • Meanings
    • Justifying means changing how elements are grouped and spaced on the main axis
    • the key:value, justify-content: flex-start (default) is used
    • flex-start means all of the flex items are grouped together at the beginning of the container
    • flex-end groupds all the items at the end of the container
  • Behaviour
    • It works when flex-direction:column; and justify-content:flex-end; so all the content is grouped at the bottom
