Notes

  • Meaning
    • To align items along the cross axis, use the align-items: stretch; property.
      This setting makes elements fill up the entire cross axis. Goes inside the container
    • If align-items: flex-start; items take the size of thier content and are at the beginning of the cross axis
    • If align-items:flex-end; items are pushed to the end of the cross axis
    • If align-items:center; items are centered
    • If the flex-direction: column; the stretch value makes items fill up the entire container width because the cross axis is horizontal
