Notes

  • Meaning
    • On the container div and display:flex; flex-wrap: wrap; align-content: stretch;
    • align-content: stretch; controls the space around wrapped rows of items across the cross axis
    • align-content: flex-start; makes wrapped rows start from the top
    • align-content: flex-end; pushes all rows to the end of the container.
    • align-content: center; groups all of the rows in the center of the main axis
    • align-content: space-around; adds space between rows across the main axis (margin)
    • align-content: space-between; adds space between rows but remove the space near the edges
