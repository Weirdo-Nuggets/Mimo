Notes

  • Meaning
    • To stop items from overflowing outside of their container use the flex-wrap: nowrap;
    • This is its default, so if items cannot shrink anymore to fit the container, they overflow
    • flex-wrap:wrap; makes items start on a new line when they don't fit anymore
    • flex-rap: wrap-reverse; makes items that don't fit go to the top of the container
    • This also works with columns and rows so flex-direction can be used
