Notes

  • Meaning- Main Axis
    • The main axis is an imaginary line that refers to teh direction and orientation of flex items,
      defined by the flex-direction
    • They are used because FlexBox is designed to work for websites in different languages
    • On the main axis, flex-direction:row shows items horizontally form left to right
      flex-direction: row-reverse; has the main axis being horizontal, going from right to left
    • On the main axis, flex-direction:column; shows items vertically, from top to bottom
      and flex-direction: column-reveerse; has the main axis vertical going from bottom to top
  • Cross Axis
    • Cross axis are the imaginary lines perpendicular to the main axis.
    • e.g the cross axis for row and row-reverse is vertical (row/row-reverse are horizontal)
