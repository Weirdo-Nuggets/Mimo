Notes

  • Meaning
    • flex-grow: 0; controls how a flex item grows across its main axis
    • The default value 0 means that the item doesn't adapt in size and is only as wide as its content
    • By setting it to a higher number, it overpowers the other item and takes up most of the remaining space
    • also adapts based on the flex-direction
    • Setting all items to flex-grow: 1; will make them all grow to equal size and fill out the container
    • Making one of the items bigger will make that item take up more space that the others
